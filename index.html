Webpage to evaluate the mean opinion score (MOS)

Many-to-one voice conversion

比馬特

Deep Learning and Media System Laboratory, CSIE

NCU Taiwan

There are two types of measurement:

  1. Naturalness : Measures speech produced similar to a human voice (not a robotic voice)
  2. Speaker similarity : Measures the similarity based on the character of the voice with the conversion target (this similarity is not related to the sentence that the utterance pair spoke)

We put four methods (A, B, C, and D) randomly to avoid tagging and be fair.

Each method consists of two baselines and two proposed methods.
